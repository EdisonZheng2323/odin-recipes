Lasagna

Description

This recipe will make your mouth water if you follow all the steps with the appropriate ingredients. It will taste just like an authentic lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake in the oven at 350 degrees F
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving
